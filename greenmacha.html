Green Macha Latte

Prep time: 5 mins

Yield: 1

Ingredients




Steps

  1. Sift your macha so it is lump-free.
  2. Spoon the macha into a large mug.
  3. Add the hot water and whisk briskly, in an up and down motion, until frothy - 30 seconds or so.
  4. Add the coconut milk and whisk until well combined.
  5. Taste and add additional water, coconut milk, and/or sweetener to your liking.
